Welcome to Crazy Gear

  • Use the arrows to move right, left, up and down
  • Right-click to rotate clockwise and left-click to rotate anticlockwise !
  • Catch the falling obstacles with the right cavity shape oriented to the top.
  • The gear has limitless capacity to store the collected obstacles
  • If the counts of collected figures form an arithmetic sequence, you score twice the total number of figures
  • If the counts of collected figures form a geometric sequence, you score 3 times the total number of figures
  • The gear empties its collection of obstacles when you score.

    • Good Luck !
